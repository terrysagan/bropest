Rodent

Among rodents in Alberta, the house mouse is the only interior rodent and the most troublesome structural pest. Other rodents live and feed on grass and small plants around structures and seldom enter inside a property. Most noticeable are voles and pocket gophers; both damage and distort lawns and landscaping:

  1. House Mice
  2. Voles
  3. Pocket Gophers
  4. Richardson Ground Squirrel; commonly called "Gopher"
